## Project Overview:

Hunger Heroes is a Jekyll-based frontend built by our team to make food donation easier, safer, and faster. The project connects people and organizations with surplus food to nearby shelters and food banks.

## Problem Statement

- 30-40% of food in USA ends up in trash
- $40 billion worth thrown away annually by supermarkets/restaurants
- Average American family wastes $1,866/year on uneaten food
- Meanwhile, millions of Americans face food insecurity daily

## Solution Overview

**For Donors:**
- Register as individual/group/business
- List excess food with zip code
- Get matched with nearby receivers
- Choose delivery or pick-up preference

**For Receivers (Shelters/Food Banks):**
- Register with organization details
- Review donation requests within 24 hours
- Accept or decline donations
- Rate food quality and ban bad actors

## Our Mission / Purpose

- To divert food from landfills
- To provide fresh food (not just canned goods)
- To serve more people experiencing food insecurity
- To encourage donation of food over disposal
